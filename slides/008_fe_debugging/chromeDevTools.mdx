## What are the Google Chrome DevTools?

* The Google Chrome Developer Tools, also known as Chrome DevTools, are web authoring and debugging tools built right into the browser.
---

## What toos do we have?

* `Device Mode`
* `Elements panel`
* `Console panel`
* `Sources panel`
* `Network panel`
* `Performance panel`
* `Memory panel`
* `Application panel`
